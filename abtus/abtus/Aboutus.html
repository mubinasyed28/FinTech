About Us

Established in 2021, the FinTech Committee at VIT College, Mumbai, stands as the sole finance-focused student body on campus for the past five years. With a legacy of excellence, we have proudly nurtured a vibrant community of over 700+ students, creating a platform that bridges theoretical knowledge with real-world financial expertise.

Our journey has been fueled by partnerships with industry leaders like Upsurge, one of our most prominent sponsors. Through their support, we’ve conducted a multitude of impactful events, from educational workshops and guest lectures to hands-on trading simulations and financial strategy competitions.

The FinTech Committee is more than just a hub for finance enthusiasts—it's a launchpad for future leaders in the financial world. We are committed to fostering financial literacy, promoting innovation in fintech, and building a strong network of students passionate about shaping the future of finance.

Join us as we continue to explore, innovate, and lead in the world of finance!
